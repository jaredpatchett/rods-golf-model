MODEL % vs MARKET %
Matchup Wave Model % Market % No-Vig % Edge Price Call
Model % is the simulated win probability for the listed (first) player. Edge = Model % − No-Vig market %. Only bet when edge clears your threshold; same-wave matchups cancel most weather/draw noise, cross-wave edges lean on the weather module. ⚠ flags a matchup worth a second look before betting — either a large PROJ skill gap (where the model's flat variance assumption is most likely to overstate edge, same issue that made Molinari/Scheffler look better than it was) or a price backed by only 1-2 books (only shown for auto/pipeline data — DraftKings-loaded matchups always show your real book). Flagged, not filtered — the call stays on the board, the judgment stays with you.
